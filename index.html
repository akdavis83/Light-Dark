The enemy tried his best and still failed, he tried fear, lies, slander, condemnation, personal attacks, he tried every tactic he had up his sleeves to shut down the gospel but none of them worked: (Ps 27:1-4) A Psalm of David. 1 The LORD is my light and my salvation; whom shall I fear? the LORD is the strength of my life; of whom shall I be afraid? 2 When the wicked, even mine enemies and my foes, came upon me to eat up my flesh, they stumbled and fell. 3 Though an host should encamp against me, my heart shall not fear: though war should rise against me, in this will I be confident. 4 One thing have I desired of the LORD, that will I seek after; that I may dwell in the house of the LORD all the days of my life, to behold the beauty of the LORD, and to enquire in his temple.
